Our Policies

Transparency. Trust. Your rights, clearly stated.

1. Privacy Policy

At ShipShop, your privacy is our priority. We collect only essential data to process your orders and improve your experience. Your personal information is kept secure and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

2. Data Usage

We use data to personalize your experience and offer relevant product recommendations. Data may be used for internal analytics, marketing, or customer service — but always with full confidentiality.

3. Return & Refund Policy

We offer a 7-day return policy on most products. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days after product inspection.

4. Shipping Policy

Orders are usually shipped within 2–3 business days. Delivery time varies by location. We partner with trusted courier services to ensure timely delivery. Shipping charges are calculated during checkout.

5. Terms of Use

By using ShipShop, you agree to our terms and conditions. Any misuse, fraudulent activity, or violation of policies may result in account suspension. Users must provide accurate information for smooth order fulfillment.

6. Contact & Support

For any policy-related questions, contact our support team via Contact Us page or email us at support@shipshop.com. We're here to help!
